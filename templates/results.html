Hi! This is my summer project and it involved me learning machine learning for something useful and meaningful.

This website uses a model I trained to detect if an Xray shows Bacterial, Viral or no signs of Pneumonia. While Pneumonia may not be prevalent in many ways it is relavant to me and many others in the world.

To see if an Xray has anything please upload the image. Please make sure it is an image file, jpeg or jpg to be exact. Furthermore, this can only take in one image at a time.

If you find this useful and would like a better way to upload many images at a time, do contact me with the contact information I provide in the about page.
